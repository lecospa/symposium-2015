Registration

Attensions (Nov 17, 2015): No more seat available for Banquet and Excursion. We still welcome you to register the symposium but please note that Banquet and Excursion are excluded.

Registration to conference requires three steps:

  1. Choose your ticket type (student or early bird) and tick box "terms of the service" and "privacy policy"
  2. Fill out the personal information
  3. Pay the registration fee by credit card
  4. An order confirmation and link for poster/talk title/abstract submission will be sent to your email later. Another email will be sent after you submit your poster/talk title/abstract. That is a passcode for revising or uploading your talk slides

Notice: LeCosPA notice that scholars who hold the mainland China passports take longer time than before to get permission to enter Taiwan, so our symposium committee decide to extend the early bird registration fee (TWD12,000) for Mainland China scholars.

Accommodation

Howard International House

Under the direct management of Howard & Resorts International, Howard International House provides you with a pleasant and modern environment. It is the nearest hotel with a 5-minute walking distance to our symposium venue. At night, you can walk to Shida Night Market or Yongkang Street to have a taste of Taiwanese cuisine. There are a variety of foods that will definitely amaze you!

Just Sleep @NTU

Just Sleep @NTU is another comfy and convenient option for you. It is just a 2-minutes walking distance to MRT (Taipei subway) and 15 minutes walking distant to our symposium venue. Just Sleep @ NTU is also close to Gongguan, one of the most popular shopping spots in Taipei. You can find a lot of shops that sells small electronic gadgets and cell phones. Sports and outfits shops are all around. And of course, the number of food stands and restaurants are out of your imagination.

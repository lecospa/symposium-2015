Transportations

Several ways get to National Taiwan University from airports are shown as below. However, the venue of our symposium is on the northwest side of campus. A ten-minutes walking distance is needed from the NTU main gate.

There are two airports in Northern Taiwan:  Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport (TPE) and Taipei Songshan Airport (TSA).  Most attendees arrive in Taiwan at TPE. Attendees from TPE can reach the symposium venue by taking taxi, high-speed rails or bus.

TSA was a domestic airport but now it has flights between Mainland China and Japan.  Therefore if you are from Mainland China or Japan, we recommend you to pick TSA.  It takes 1/2 time to reach our symposium venue compared to TPE.
